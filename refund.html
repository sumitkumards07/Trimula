Refund Policy

Understanding our refund process

1. Eligibility for Refund

Refunds are only applicable for bookings cancelled within the timeframe specified in our Cancellation Policy. Processing fees may be deducted.

2. Processing Time

Once a refund is approved, it will be processed within 7-10 working days. The amount will be credited back to the original payment source.

3. Non-Refundable Items

Early check-out charges and certain promotional bookings may be non-refundable.

4. Contact for Refunds

For any refund-related queries, please contact us at help@panchajanyam.com with your booking details.
