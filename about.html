About Us

Your Spiritual Home in the Heart of Tirumala

Welcome to Panchajanyam Guest House

Panchajanyam Guest House is dedicated to providing pilgrims and travelers with a serene, comfortable, and spiritual stay in Tirumala. Located within easy reach of the sacred temple, we blend traditional hospitality with modern amenities.

Our goal is to ensure that your pilgrimage is as comfortable as possible, allowing you to focus on your spiritual journey. With clean rooms, friendly staff, and a peaceful environment, we have become a preferred choice for devotees from across the country.
