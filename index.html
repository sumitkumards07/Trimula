About Panchajanyam Guest House

Located in Balajy Colony, Tirumala, Panchajanyam Guest House is a trusted and serene retreat for devotees visiting the Sri Venkateswara Swamy Temple. We are just a short walk from the main temple and Free Aadhaar Card Darshan counters.

We offer a range of rooms from budget-friendly Non-AC rooms to spacious AC Family Suites and Hall rooms for large pilgrim groups, all maintained to the highest standards of cleanliness and comfort.
